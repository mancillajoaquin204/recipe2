Classic Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:
12 oz (340g) pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your favorite)
2 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
1 tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
Fresh basil leaves, torn or chopped
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
1 tbsp butter (optional for richness)
Instructions:
Cook the pasta:

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.
Make the sauce:

While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant, but not browned.
Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar (if using). Let the sauce simmer on low for 10-15 minutes.
Combine pasta and sauce:

Add the drained pasta to the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up. Stir in the butter if you want extra creaminess.
Serve:

Toss the pasta with fresh basil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes if you want a kick.
Top with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
Enjoy your homemade pasta!

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
For the Chicken Marinade:

1 lb (450g) boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 inch ginger, minced
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp chili powder or paprika
Salt to taste
For the Sauce:

2 tbsp butter or oil
1 large onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 inch ginger, minced
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp chili powder or paprika
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut milk (for dairy-free)
1 tbsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh cilantro (optional, for garnish)
Instructions:
Marinate the Chicken:

In a bowl, mix the yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
Add the chicken pieces and coat well with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for more flavor).
Cook the Chicken:

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) or heat a grill pan on medium heat.
Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers (if grilling) or spread them on a baking sheet.
Grill or roast the chicken for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
Make the Sauce:

In a large pan, heat the butter or oil over medium heat.
Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
Stir in the garlic and ginger, cooking until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder, and cook for another minute to release the spices' flavors.
Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add the cream and sugar (if using) and mix well. Let the sauce simmer for another 5 minutes.
Combine:

Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and stir to coat the chicken in the creamy tomato sauce.
Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
Serve:

Garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot with rice, naan, or your preferred side.
Enjoy your delicious homemade Chicken Tikka Masala!
